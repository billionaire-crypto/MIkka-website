About

Artist &
Educator

Mikka Chinbaatar is a San Francisco–based pianist whose artistry bridges the classical tradition and the rich musical heritage of Mongolia. Equally at home on the concert stage and in the teaching studio, she brings a rare depth of musicianship to every performance and lesson.

As a collaborative pianist, Mikka has partnered with instrumentalists and vocalists across the Bay Area, bringing sensitivity and precision to chamber music and vocal repertoire. Her work as a yatga soloist — performing on the traditional Mongolian zither — reflects her commitment to preserving and sharing her cultural roots through music.

In her teaching practice, Mikka guides students of all levels with warmth and rigor, emphasizing musical expression alongside technical mastery.
